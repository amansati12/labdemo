About Us

Our Story

Serving the Community with Precision & Care

Suvidha Sonography & X Ray was established with a single mission — to provide high-quality, affordable diagnostic imaging services to every patient who walks through our doors.

Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted diagnostic centres in the region, serving thousands of patients with accurate reports and compassionate care. Our name "Suvidha" means convenience — and that's exactly what we deliver.

From routine X-rays to complex color Doppler studies, our experienced radiologists and trained technicians ensure every scan is performed with precision and every report is delivered on time.
