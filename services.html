Diagnostic Services

Everything You Need, All in One Place

We offer a full range of imaging services performed by qualified professionals using advanced equipment.

Obstetric Sonography

Comprehensive ultrasound monitoring throughout pregnancy to ensure the health and development of mother and baby.

  • Early pregnancy scan (6–10 weeks)
  • NT scan / Nuchal Translucency (11–14 weeks)
  • Anomaly scan (18–22 weeks)
  • Growth scan & fetal well-being
  • Placenta and amniotic fluid assessment
  • Biophysical profile (BPP)
Book Appointment

Color Doppler Study

Advanced Doppler imaging to evaluate blood flow in vessels and organs for accurate vascular diagnosis.

  • Fetal Doppler (umbilical, MCA, ductus venosus)
  • Carotid Doppler
  • Renal Doppler
  • Peripheral vascular Doppler
  • Hepatic & portal vein Doppler
  • Scrotal Doppler
Book Appointment

Digital X-Ray

High-definition digital radiography for fast, clear imaging with minimal radiation exposure.

  • Chest X-Ray (PA / AP view)
  • Spine X-Ray (cervical, dorsal, lumbar)
  • Bone & joint X-Ray
  • Skull & facial bones
  • Abdomen X-Ray
  • KUB (Kidney, Ureter, Bladder)
Book Appointment

Abdominal Sonography

Detailed ultrasound evaluation of all abdominal organs for accurate diagnosis of various conditions.

  • Liver, gallbladder & bile ducts
  • Pancreas & spleen
  • Kidneys & urinary bladder
  • Aorta & inferior vena cava
  • Detection of ascites & masses
  • Post-operative follow-up
Book Appointment

Gynaecological Sonography

Pelvic and transvaginal ultrasound for comprehensive evaluation of the female reproductive system.

  • Uterus & ovarian assessment
  • Follicular monitoring (IVF support)
  • PCOD / PCOS evaluation
  • Fibroid & cyst detection
  • Endometrial thickness measurement
  • Transvaginal sonography (TVS)
Book Appointment

Musculoskeletal Sonography

High-resolution ultrasound for soft tissue, joint and muscle evaluation in orthopaedic conditions.

  • Shoulder, knee & hip joint USG
  • Tendon & ligament assessment
  • Soft tissue mass evaluation
  • Rotator cuff injury detection
  • Plantar fasciitis assessment
  • Guided aspiration support
Book Appointment

Thyroid & Neck Sonography

Detailed imaging of the thyroid gland and neck structures for accurate evaluation.

  • Thyroid gland size & echotexture
  • Nodule characterization (TIRADS)
  • Parathyroid assessment
  • Cervical lymph node evaluation
  • Salivary gland imaging
Book Appointment

Small Parts Sonography

High-frequency ultrasound for detailed imaging of superficial structures.

  • Scrotal & testicular USG
  • Breast sonography
  • Eye (ocular) sonography
  • Superficial lymph nodes
  • Skin & subcutaneous lesions
